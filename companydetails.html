Questions to Answer Before Approaching Investors

 

Do you have a comprehensive business plan?

A clear and comprehensive business plan will enable the investors to understand your company today and its future goals which will convince them to invest in your company or not.

Do you have skilled and permanent employees? Have you provided Directors' profiles?

These questions will help you to know the stability of your team(workers) and if you have all profiles of board directors before meeting any investors. This will increase the investor' trust in your company.

Are you operating in a high growth industry? Have you met the needs of your Key Customers?

Every investor is interested in investing in industries that have high growth so that they may not encouter any loss or delay. Moreover, meeting your customers' needs should be your main goal in order to make your business a success.

Have you completed your competitor' analysis including identifying barriers to entry?

You need to know your compentitors, their strengths and weaknesses. This will serve as an awakener in order to know what to do in your company and to identify challenges that may hinder you from progressing.

Have you identified the level of funding required e.g venture capital and/or bank borrowing?

Knowing the level of funding you need will help you to be very specific with your investors by telling them the exact amount of money you need. It is also better to have a fair idea of how you will get capital for your company, hence you will be sure if you need total funding or half before you meet your investors.
